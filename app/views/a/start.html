About this assessment

This assessment asks you to agree or disagree with statements about yourself.

We’ll use your answers to suggest types of jobs you might be suited to.

How long will it take

There are 40 questions.

The assessment will take 5 minutes to complete.

You can save your assessment to complete it or look at your results again another time.

How to respond

A lot of the statements are about how you are at work. If you have not had a job yet, think how you are with your friends and family.

Some of the statements are very similar. Try to answer honestly, even if you think you’ve see a statement before.

You will get more accurate results if you respond to every statement. But if you cannot respond, you can choose ‘This does not apply to me’.
