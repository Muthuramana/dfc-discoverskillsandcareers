About this assessment

This assessment asks you to agree or disagree with statements about yourself.

We’ll use your answers to suggest specific job roles you might be suited to.

How long it will take

There are 71 questions.

The assessment could take 10-15 minutes.

It will take longer than this if you’re using assistive technologies, for example a screen reader or screen magnifier.

You can save your assessment if you want to complete it later, or look at your results again another time.

How to respond

A lot of the statements are about how you are at work. If you have not had a job yet, think how you are with your friends and family.
